-Whenever you're commanded to move the dog forwards, always try to use the service 
to move the dog a certain distance and same with rotate. 
- For the distance variable, distance = 5 is 5cm
- This is the format for the service call:
```{
  "request": {
    "distance": 10,
    "speed": 0.5
  },
  "service_name": "/puppy/move_distance_trigger",
  "service_type": "puppy_control_msgs/srv/MoveDistanceTrigger",
  "timeout": 15
}```
